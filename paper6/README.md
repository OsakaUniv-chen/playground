# Paper 6 — PhD Capstone

The integrative capstone paper of the PhD: it unifies two prior research fields into one real-world human–robot interaction study and serves as the thesis backbone. **No new algorithm — the contribution is integration.**

- **Field B (Papers 1–2):** social-HRI policy learning — IRL→RL android receptionist, field-deployed in a shopping mall (decide/act layer).
- **Field A (Papers 3–5):** anticipatory perception — PSSP predicts future sound sources to drive anticipatory robot head motion (perceive/attend layer).

**Thesis:** one autonomous social robot that perceives anticipatorily (A) and acts via a learned policy (B), validated in a real-world field run.
